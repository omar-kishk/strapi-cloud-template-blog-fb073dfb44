## Homepage Copy

### Hero Section

**Prompt Template:**
```
Create compelling hero section copy for the AlThuraya marketing website homepage. The copy should:

Context:
- Business: {business_description}
- Target Audience: {target_audience}
- Unique Value Proposition: {value_proposition}

Requirements:
- Write a powerful headline (max 10 words)
- Write a supporting subheadline (max 25 words)
- Suggest 1-2 CTA button texts
- Tone: {professional/friendly/innovative}
- Language: {English/Arabic/Both}

Focus on emotions: {trust/excitement/urgency/innovation}
```

**Example Usage:**
```
Create compelling hero section copy for the AlThuraya marketing website homepage. The copy should:

Context:
- Business: Full-service digital marketing agency specializing in SEO, social media, and content marketing
- Target Audience: SMEs and startups in Saudi Arabia looking to grow their online presence
- Unique Value Proposition: Data-driven strategies with measurable ROI

Requirements:
- Write a powerful headline (max 10 words)
- Write a supporting subheadline (max 25 words)
- Suggest 1-2 CTA button texts
- Tone: Professional but approachable
- Language: Both English and Arabic

Focus on emotions: Trust and innovation
```

**Expected Output Format:**
```
Headline (EN): Transform Your Digital Presence, Grow Your Business
Headline (AR): حوّل حضورك الرقمي، وطوّر أعمالك

Subheadline (EN): Partner with AlThuraya for data-driven digital marketing strategies that deliver measurable results and sustainable growth.
Subheadline (AR): شارك مع الثريا للحصول على استراتيجيات تسويق رقمي قائمة على البيانات تحقق نتائج قابلة للقياس ونمواً مستداماً.

Primary CTA (EN): Start Your Growth Journey
Primary CTA (AR): ابدأ رحلة نموك

Secondary CTA (EN): View Our Services
Secondary CTA (AR): تصفح خدماتنا
```

## Blog Posts

### Blog Post Creation

**Prompt Template:**
```
Write a comprehensive blog post about {topic}.

Content Brief:
- Topic: {topic}
- Target Keywords: {primary_keyword, secondary_keywords}
- Target Audience: {audience_description}
- Reading Level: {beginner/intermediate/advanced}
- Word Count: {target_word_count}
- Tone: {informative/conversational/authoritative}
- Language: {English/Arabic/Both}

Structure:
1. SEO Title (60 characters max)
2. Meta Description (155 characters max)
3. Introduction (hook + problem + solution preview)
4. Main Content (3-5 H2 sections with subsections)
5. Actionable Tips/Best Practices
6. Conclusion with CTA
7. FAQ Section (3-5 questions)

Include:
- Real examples or case studies
- Statistics or data points
- Internal link suggestions (to relevant service pages)
- External link suggestions (authoritative sources)
- Image/visual suggestions with alt text
- Call-to-action at the end

SEO Requirements:
- Include primary keyword in H1, first paragraph, and conclusion
- Natural keyword placement throughout
- Related keywords and LSI terms
- Schema markup suggestions (Article/BlogPosting)
```

**Example Usage:**
```
Write a comprehensive blog post about the latest SEO trends in 2024.

Content Brief:
- Topic: SEO Trends 2024 for Saudi Arabian Businesses
- Target Keywords: "SEO trends 2024", "Saudi Arabia SEO", "search engine optimization trends"
- Target Audience: Marketing managers and business owners in KSA
- Reading Level: Intermediate
- Word Count: 2000-2500 words
- Tone: Informative and actionable
- Language: English (Arabic version to follow)

Structure:
1. SEO Title (60 characters max)
2. Meta Description (155 characters max)
3. Introduction (hook + problem + solution preview)
4. Main Content (3-5 H2 sections with subsections)
5. Actionable Tips/Best Practices
6. Conclusion with CTA
7. FAQ Section (3-5 questions)

Include:
- Real examples from Saudi market
- Latest statistics from Google/Moz/SEMrush
- Internal link suggestions (to our SEO service page)
- External link suggestions (Google Search Central, Search Engine Journal)
- Image suggestions: infographics, screenshots, charts
- Call-to-action: Free SEO audit offer

SEO Requirements:
- Include primary keyword in H1, first paragraph, and conclusion
- Natural keyword placement throughout
- Related keywords: "Google algorithm", "search rankings", "organic traffic"
- Schema markup: Article schema with author, publish date, and image
```

```
- Keep SEO keywords: {keyword_list}
- Preserve formatting (headings, bullets, etc.)
- Adapt idioms and expressions appropriately
- Optimize for {RTL/LTR} reading
- Match character limits if specified: {limits}

Original Content:
"""
{content_to_translate}
"""

Additional Notes:
- Brand name "AlThuraya" remains in {English/Arabic/Both}
- Specific terms to keep: {terms_to_preserve}
- Cultural considerations: {considerations}
```

## Best Practices for Using These Prompts

1. **Be Specific**: Fill in all context fields with detailed information
2. **Iterate**: Generate multiple variations and refine
3. **Test**: A/B test different versions to see what resonates
4. **Localize**: Don't just translate; adapt culturally for Arabic content
5. **Optimize**: Always include relevant keywords naturally
6. **Measure**: Track performance of content and optimize based on data
7. **Stay On-Brand**: Ensure all content aligns with AlThuraya brand voice
8. **Update Regularly**: Refresh content to keep it current and relevant

## Notes

- Always fact-check statistics and claims
- Ensure all testimonials are from real clients (or clearly marked as examples)
- Keep brand guidelines and tone consistent across all content
- Review content for cultural sensitivity, especially when translating
- Test readability scores for target audience level
- Include clear CTAs in all marketing content
