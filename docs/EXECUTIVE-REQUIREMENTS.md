# Executive Requirements (BAB Pharma Website)

- Lead generation growth (contacts +200%, partnerships +150%, downloads +300%)
- Trust and credibility via compliance transparency (SFDA, ISO, GMP, CE)
- Showcase of fast growth, import/distribution network, and manufacturing vision
- AR-first bilingual experience with RTL optimization

- Product portfolio with 3D visualization, specs, regulatory docs
- Partner & customer portals (role-based access)
- Compliance center with versioned documents and approvals

## Guardrails
- Regulatory content governance and auditability
- Security-by-default: HTTPS, CSP, authN/Z, RLS at DB layer
- Structured content in CMS with localization

## Phased Delivery
- Phase 1: Homepage, core IA, product catalog, contact flows
- Phase 2: 3D manufacturing tour, supply chain viz, financial highlights
- Phase 3: Portals, analytics, optimization, compliance automation
