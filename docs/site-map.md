## User Journeys
- Healthcare Provider
  1) Products → Filter by therapeutic area → View product → Download specs → Contact Sales
- Government (NUPCO)
  1) Compliance Center → Certifications & docs → Tenders → Participation info → Contact
- International Supplier
  1) Partners → Capabilities → Onboarding form → Document upload → Review status
- Pharmacy
  1) Products → Availability/stock (portal) → Place order/request → Delivery tracking

## Page-Level Sections
- Home
  - Hero (video/3D), metrics, interactive SA map
  - Import, Distribution, Manufacturing, Innovation blocks
  - Featured products, testimonials, CTAs
- Import & Distribution
  - World partners map, cold-chain visualization, case studies
  - Compliance certifications, logistics demo
- Manufacturing Excellence
  - 3D facility tour, process animations, QC lab
  - Equipment, tech stack, environmental controls
- Products (Listing)
  - Filters (type/therapeutic/dosage), search, tags
  - Cards with highlights and quick actions
- Product Detail
  - Specs, 3D model/media, regulatory docs, related products
  - Inquiry form, downloadable leaflets/IFU/SDS/COA (if public)
- Sales & Distribution
  - Coverage map, territories, sales directory, NUPCO partnership
- Financial Highlights
  - Public KPIs, growth visuals (no sensitive data)
- Supply Chain
  - Payment cycles, multi-company coordination, inventory views (demo)
- Partners
  - International/local portals, onboarding, training resources
- Compliance Center
  - Certifications browser, policy center, document repository
- About
  - Vision/mission, leadership, facilities, sustainability
- Contact
  - Forms (sales/general/regulatory), locations, support

## Localization & Accessibility
- Default Arabic (RTL) with full layout mirroring
- Toggle to English (LTR), remember preference
- WCAG 2.1 AA targets across all pages
